How it started...

Our journey started in July, 2021

SoloSave was born from the shared wanderlust of two friends, Alex and Mia, who dreamt of turning solo travel into a collective adventure. Fueled by their own experiences of the transformative power of exploration, they envisioned a platform where like-minded individuals could connect, share expenses, and embark on incredible journeys together. With a passion for fostering a global community of solo travelers, Alex and Mia founded SoloSave, turning their vision into a reality that continues to unite adventurous souls around the world.
